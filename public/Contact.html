Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

📅 How do I book one-day-old chicks?

You can book chicks via our Booking Page. Choose your preferred bird type, quantity, and delivery date.

💰 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa and bank transfers. Payment is required before confirmation.

🚚 When will my chicks be delivered?

Delivery is done on the selected date. We recommend booking at least 7 days in advance.

🐥 What is the maximum number of chicks I can purchase?

There is no strict limit, but bulk orders (500+ chicks) require advance confirmation for availability. Please contact us for large-scale purchases.

🔄 Do you offer refunds or cancellations after booking?

Once a booking is confirmed, cancellations are not allowed. However, you may reschedule your delivery date within 24 hours of booking.
